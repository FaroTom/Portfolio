About Me
Hi, I am a Front-end developer based in Aschaffenburg, Germany. My expertise lies in developing web applications with the use of JavaScript and Angular. Feel free to browse through my portfolio of public projects, which showcases a range of responsive websites, business applications, and games.
Ever since I can remember, I have been downright enthusiastic about all kinds of technologies. This eventually led to me writing my first lines of code shortly after graduating from high school and developing a passion for it. In order to turn the initially smaller projects, which were based on various YouTube tutorials, into larger, more diverse projects, I decided to delve deeper into the subject matter. This is how I became aware of the possibility of further training as a front-end developer at the Developer Academy in Munich, which offered a professional approach to acquiring the necessary skills to gain a foothold as a front-end developer.
I have developed several user-friendly, visually appealing applications and landing pages and am very enthusiastic to take the next step as a frontend developer to use my skills to contribute to the success of your business.
