About Me
Hi, I am a Front-end developer based in Aschaffenburg, Germany. My expertise lies in developing web applications with the use of JavaScript and Angular. Feel free to browse through my portfolio of public projects, which showcases a range of responsive websites, business applications, and games.
During my time in marketing, I was entrusted with the task of translating a website into English together with one of the developers - my first contact with programming and shortly afterwards I knew that I wanted to pursue a career as a developer. To achieve my goal, I decided to join the Developer Akademie, where I was given the opportunity to develop several projects, both in teams and on my own, and was also able to improve thanks to regular feedback and code reviews from experienced developers.
I have developed user-friendly, visually appealing applications, games and landing pages. I am now confident in my ability to contribute to your team as a Front-End Developer and eager and highly motivated to continue learning and growing, both on a professional and personal level.
